One Hit Wonders Over Time

Writeup:

This is a visualization of one-hit wonders by year. You can select any given year by clicking on the year bar at the bottom. The graph shows the path that songs took on the top charts. By hovering over the lines you can see what one-hit wonder the song was and its rise and fall in the Billboard top 100. The different colors of each lines represent the genre of the song. To make this visualization easier to visually understand we only included the top 5 genres of one-hit wonders.
